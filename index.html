The mission of the Foundation Fighting Blindess is to rapidly drive the research that will lead to preventions, treatments and cures for retinal degenerative diseases - including Stargardt disease, retinitis pigmenotsa, Usher syndrome and macular degeneration. Together, these blinding conditions affect more than 50 million people throughtout the world. In support of this mission, the Foundation has created the Retinal Degeneration Fund (RD Fund) to help accelerate life-changing outcomes for people with retinal degenerations through direct mission related investments in therapeutic companies.
